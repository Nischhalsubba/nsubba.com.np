Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process?

I follow a double-diamond approach: Discover, Define, Develop, Deliver. I start by understanding the core problem, move to wireframes and prototypes, and finish with pixel-perfect UI and documentation.

Do you do development?

I am a designer who codes. I can build front-end interfaces (HTML/CSS/JS) to ensure design fidelity, but for complex backend logic, I collaborate with engineering teams.

What are your rates?

My rates depend on the project scope and complexity. I offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainers for ongoing design system support.

Are you available for full-time roles?

I am currently open to discussing remote full-time opportunities or long-term contract roles in the Fintech or Web3 space.
