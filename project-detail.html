Fintech Mobile App

Nova Financial

Redefining the mobile banking experience for a new generation of investors.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Timeline

3 Months

Client

Nova Corp

The Challenge

Nova Financial wanted to expand their user base to include Gen Z investors. However, their legacy application was cluttered, slow, and full of financial jargon that intimidated new users. The goal was to simplify the interface without losing advanced functionality required by power users.

My Approach

  • Research: Conducted user interviews with 15 participants aged 18-25 to understand investing habits.
  • Audit: Analyzed key competitors (Robinhood, Revolut, Monzo) to identify UX gaps.
  • Wireframing: Created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on a "One action per screen" philosophy.
  • UI Design: Developed a dark-mode first system to align with modern aesthetic preferences and battery saving needs.

The Solution

I designed a dashboard that prioritizes net worth visualization and quick actions. We moved complex trading tools to a secondary layer, keeping the primary interface clean. We also introduced "Nova Academy", a gamified learning hub that rewards users for learning financial literacy.
