Fintech Mobile App

Nova Financial

Redefining the mobile banking experience for a new generation of investors.

The Challenge

Nova Financial wanted to expand their user base to include Gen Z investors. However, their legacy application was cluttered, slow, and full of financial jargon that intimidated new users. The drop-off rate during onboarding was nearly 60%.

My Approach

  • Conducted user interviews with 15 participants aged 18-25.
  • Analyzed key competitors to identify UX gaps.
  • Proposed a "Progressive Disclosure" model.

The Outcome

After a 3-month redesign and development cycle, we launched the V2 app to a test group of 10,000 users. Retention increased by 15%.
